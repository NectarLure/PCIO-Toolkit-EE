PCIO Staged Capability Model

P: Perception
Data capture, sensing, equipment-state visibility, and completeness of basic records.
C: Connectivity
System connection, data integration, and mapping between business processes and assets or areas.
I: Insight
Risk identification, anomaly interpretation, KPI suggestions, and decision-support information.
O: Optimization
Option comparison, ROI scenario estimation, PDCA retest planning, and human confirmation.

Diagnosis-to-PDCA Loop

DiagnosisCollect profile and survey responses
RiskGenerate candidate risks from rules
KPISuggest trackable indicators
ROI ScenarioCompare assumptions and options
PDCAPlan retests and human confirmation

The loop is a decision-support logic. It is not evidence of longitudinal improvement.

Toolkit Module Structure

Module Function Outputs Evidence Boundary
Organisation Profile Define context, scope, and diagnostic assumptions. Context summary and applicability boundary. Provides diagnostic context, not performance evidence.
Survey and PCIO Mapping Map item responses to PCIO capability layers. Layer scores, baseline gaps, diagnostic prompts. Real T0 data support baseline diagnosis, not longitudinal change.
Risk Matrix Generate candidate risks from capability gaps and context. Risk list, priority level, human confirmation prompts. Rules require expert review and field confirmation.
KPI Suggestions Translate diagnostic issues into trackable indicators. KPI name, data definition, follow-up frequency. Supports KPI definition, not KPI improvement.
ROI Scenario Support option comparison and assumption discussion. Scenario parameters and sensitivity assumptions. Does not represent realised financial return.
Report and PDCA Produce diagnostic reports and retest plans. Summary, risks, KPIs, action plan. A retest plan is not a retest result.

Design Principles

ID Design Principle Meaning Review Question
DP1Prioritise foundational capability before optimisationAssess perception and connectivity before advanced insight or optimisation.Does this sequence fit resource-constrained organisations?
DP2Evidence-linked diagnosis and decision supportRisks and KPIs should trace back to items, context, or rules.Are the outputs sufficiently traceable?
DP3Low-cost modular deploymentKeep the tool lightweight, local, and separable.Are module boundaries practical for SMEs?
DP4Human-machine collaboration and confirmationKeep human judgement in risk, KPI, and ROI interpretation.Which outputs require expert confirmation?
DP5PDCA-based retestingGenerate retest plans without replacing real follow-up evaluation.Are retest intervals and KPI definitions reasonable?
DP6Context adaptation with cross-case comparabilityMaintain a common PCIO logic while allowing scenario-specific expression.Which contextual variables should be made explicit?
