Candidate Risks

  • Inconsistent multi-area interface status and task records.
  • Insufficient evidence chain among loading/unloading tasks, area status, and alarm records.
  • Weak linkage between trend data and abnormal-event review records.
  • Unstable mapping among areas, assets, and tasks.
  • Overreliance on tacit operator experience with insufficient confirmation records.

These are anonymised candidate diagnostic outputs and require confirmation by storage/operations experts.

KPI Suggestions

  • Valid record rate for key operating data.
  • Completeness of area-asset-loading task mapping.
  • Completeness of alarm confirmation and closure records.
  • Trend-data availability and abnormal-event review completion rate.
  • Completeness of human confirmation records and retest-item completion rate.

These KPIs are follow-up tracking suggestions and do not indicate current operational improvement.
