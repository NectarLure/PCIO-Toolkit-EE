# PCIO Toolkit DSR Expert Review Form

This blank form supports formative expert review of PCIO Toolkit as a Design Science Research artifact. Please provide a 1-5 Likert score and a short reason for each item.

Scale:

- 1 = Strongly disagree / clearly insufficient
- 2 = Disagree / several weaknesses
- 3 = Neutral / acceptable but needs revision
- 4 = Agree / reasonably sound
- 5 = Strongly agree / very sound

Evidence boundaries:

- Real cases are T0 baseline diagnosis cases only and do not represent post-deployment improvement.
- Synthetic cases are used only for workflow and rule-response demonstration.
- Automated tests support technical correctness, functional completeness, and module consistency only.
- ROI is a scenario-estimation tool and does not represent realised financial return.
- This is a blank form and contains no expert evaluation result.
