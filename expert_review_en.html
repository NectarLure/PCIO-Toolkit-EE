Scoring Boundary

Please assess PCIO Toolkit as a DSR artifact for design rationality and formative applicability. Do not interpret T0 baseline cases as post-deployment performance evidence, and do not interpret ROI scenarios as realised financial returns.

Reviewer Background (optional)
Likert Scale: 1=Strongly disagree, 5=Strongly agree
Evaluation Dimension and Explanation 12345
Rationality of the PCIO four-layer model
Assess whether Perception, Connectivity, Insight, and Optimization represent a reasonable staged capability logic for smart-manufacturing diagnosis.
Coverage of diagnostic items
Assess whether the items cover data capture, system connection, diagnostic insight, optimisation loops, organisational support, and human confirmation.
Rationality of risk rules
Assess whether the risk matrix generates explainable, confirmable candidate risks from PCIO gaps and contextual features.
Executability of KPI suggestions
Assess whether KPI suggestions have clear definitions, feasible data sources, responsibility boundaries, and retest practicality.
Cautious expression of ROI scenarios
Assess whether ROI is clearly framed as scenario estimation and option comparison, not realised benefit or performance validation.
Understandability of the report
Assess whether diagnostic results, risks, KPIs, ROI scenarios, and PDCA plans are understandable for non-research users.
Fit for process-manufacturing enterprises
Assess whether the tool fits batch, asset, quality, process-control, and maintenance diagnosis in process manufacturing.
Fit for tank-area storage and operations
Assess whether the tool fits area, asset, loading/unloading, alarm trend, and human-confirmation diagnosis.
Clarity of evidence boundaries
Assess whether the site distinguishes automated tests, synthetic cases, real T0 cases, and future longitudinal validation.
Overall practical potential
Assess the artifact's potential value for organisational diagnosis after further expert review, field trial, and longitudinal retesting.
